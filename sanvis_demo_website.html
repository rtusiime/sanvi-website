Pumpkin Cannoli Recipe

Ingredients

1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
1/2 cup canned pumpkin puree
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup finely chopped nuts (pistachios or pecans work well)
12 premade cannoli shells

Instructions

1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, pumpkin puree, confectioners' sugar, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla extract.
2. Mix with a rubber spatula until the filling is smooth.
3. Transfer the pumpkin filling into a piping bag fitted with a star or round tip. Alternatively, you can use a plastic bag with one corner snipped off.
4. Pipe the filling into each of the cannoli shells, filling them from both ends until they are full.
5. Sprinkle the finely chopped nuts over the filled ends of the cannoli.
6. Serve immediately to enjoy the crisp texture of the shells.

Tips

If you don't have a piping bag, a resealable plastic bag works just as well. Simply snip off one of the bottom corners after filling it.
For an extra touch, you can drizzle melted white or dark chocolate over the cannoli.
To keep the shells from getting soggy, fill them just before serving
